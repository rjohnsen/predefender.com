Summary

Roger is the author of “The Huntbook by Predefender ”, a comprehensive guide designed to support cybersecurity professionals in threat hunting and incident response. With a background in Information Security Consulting, he specializes in strengthening Blue Team capabilities, developing threat hunting strategies, and refining software development and quality assurance processes. As the Head of Security Operations at Orkla IT, Roger applies his expertise to protect the organization’s digital assets and enhance its security posture. A passionate Capture The Flag (CTF) participant, Roger actively competes in platforms like the SANS Holiday Hack Challenge, Hack The Box, and TryHackMe. His dedication to these activities earned him a runner-up position for the best “Technical Answer” in the SANS Holiday Hack Challenge 2021, along with a challenge coin for his achievement. Driven by a commitment to quality, Roger leverages his extensive knowledge of system development and professional experience to tackle complex cybersecurity challenges with precision
